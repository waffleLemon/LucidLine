# App & Privacy Policy

LucidLine respects your privacy. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect personal information.

### Data Collection
LucidLine does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 

### Local Data
App settings and preferences you configure are stored locally on your device. This data is used only to maintain your preferences and ensure the app functions properly.
The data is never transmitted outside your device, and the developer does not have access to this data.
